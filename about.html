About Me

More of an insight into me as a person and a trainer

Personal Background

Interesting fact about me, I have type one diabetes.

One thing I’ve never had an issue with is my motivation and dedication to helping others to enjoy training and achieve their goals. I want to achieve the goals of others as much as they want to achieve it, purely for selfish reasons. I crave seeing the satisfaction on their face and how much I can change their life, I’m a trainer who cares and actually wants to help.

I have an addiction to learning, which makes my career even more satisfying, since becoming a PT I have taken/attended 2+ additional courses/seminars on nutrition, training, & coaching each year. My most notable achievement to date is becoming a level 2 Strength coach after studying under the worlds leading strength coach Sebastian Oreb (Australian Strength Coach). I also attended a programme design seminar of Sebastian’s a year later.

Why I love what I do

  • I love the feeling that changing somebody’s life gives me.
  • I love teaching & educating.
  • I love learning and, in this job, you never stop learning.
  • I don’t like all the myths in the industry, it is my goal to make eating and training simpler and more enjoyable.

Career Achievements

  • Achieving Level 2 Strength Coach Certificate.
  • Reversing early onset type 2 diabetes in my client.
  • Proud of the amount of time and money that I invest - and continue to invest - yearly into my growth and education.
  • Coached multiple clients to become stronger than me.

Qualifications I Have

  • Level 4 Strength and Conditioning
  • Level 2 ASC Certified Strength Coach
  • Level 3 Personal Trainer
  • Metabolic Conditioning Training
