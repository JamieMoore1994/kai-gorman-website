What was your reason/need for contacting Kai?

I had gained a bit of fat over university, never been strong or fit (only ever skinny or chubby), and I wanted to challenge myself into improving my health. I also wanted to look better aesthetically for vanity reasons.

How has Kai helped to improve your knowledge on training & nutrition?

He always went into detail with me about the scientific reasons why I would do certain exercises or eat certain foods. He hugely increased my gym confidence and now I really feel like I can push on with my fitness, because he's equipped me with the base knowledge and understanding I need to learn more by myself. I've learned a lot about nutrients and how/when to eat to improve at an optimal rate. I also got some valuable general life advice along the way.

What did you enjoy the most about training with Kai?

Kai really pushed me out of my comfort zone and didn't accept anything less than maximum effort in each session. The feeling of accomplishment when I see the weight I can lift increase week on week is just the best, and after a while that becomes so addictive. The gym is now my stress relief and the best part of my day - even the leg days! There is something really satisfying by the thought that my body is literally becoming more powerful as I continue.

Why would you recommend Kai to somebody who might be on the fence or considering signing up with Kai?

Go for it. He does his research, knows what he is talking about and most importantly he passes that knowledge on to you. Actually at his own detriment, because the only reason I'm no longer with Kai is because I feel like he's taught me so much I can continue my fitness journey on my own! Also, personally I found him very easy to get along with even from day one, and looking around the gym at other personal trainers working with clients I always found myself thinking I'd got the best one.

What are the main benefits you have received from having Kai as your coach?

I feel like a different person now - my confidence has soared, I feel fitter, healthier and eat a better diet. This really helped to improve my confidence and that dramatically improved my life outside the gym too. Maybe the best part was that Kai seemed to be genuinely proud of my progress, which helped me to be proud of myself too. I could talk about how my body has improved or how many kg I can lift now, but this whole new source of self worth is probably the most valuable thing I gained.

Would you recommend Kai to a friend? If so, why?

Absolutely. I recommend personal training to everyone I know now. It's expensive but I think for my physical and mental health, what I gained was of far higher value than money. I feel like I've been picked up and set on a path towards a much healthier future. I couldn't be more happy with my progress in just 6 months. With all the knowledge and experience Kai has given to me, I can't wait to see what I can do in another 6.
