My Specialties Areas

  • Strength Training

    Strength training- for maximal strength development, train to increase the weight lifted on the gym floor while maintaining injury free. This can be seen as functional fitness and will provide you a better quality of life.

    Rehabilitation- rehabilitation can be something that I can programme into somebodies training along side other goals, if you have any aches or pains (typically lower back/shoulders) I will work along side you to address these issues and have you moving pain free again..

    To lose body fat you need to burn more calories than you consume, sounds simple? But that’s not always easy to do, work with me to find the best and easiest way for YOU to cut down your calories and lose body fat.
